# Smart Pesticide Assistant - Geographical Expansion Plan

## Vision

Expand the Smart Pesticide Assistant across India by supporting multiple languages and regional crops.

---

## Current Coverage

Currently supporting:

- English
- Telugu
- Hindi

---

## Phase 1

Target States

- Andhra Pradesh
- Telangana

Activities

- College demonstrations
- Farmer awareness programs
- Local testing

---

## Phase 2

Expand to

- Karnataka
- Tamil Nadu
- Maharashtra

Activities

- Agricultural universities
- Farmer Producer Organizations
- Extension officers

---

## Phase 3

Expand across India through collaborations with:

- Agricultural universities
- NGOs
- Government agriculture departments
- Farmer organizations

---

## User Onboarding

1. Open the application.
2. Select preferred language.
3. Upload crop leaf image.
4. Receive disease diagnosis.
5. View pesticide recommendation.
6. Follow prevention tips.
7. Use AI chatbot.

---
